## English Version

### Purpose of the Study

This study investigates how users experience emotional engagement when interacting with an AI-driven character through a simulated phone interface. The research is conducted as part of a master's thesis at Aalto University, School of Arts, Design and Architecture.

### What Participation Involves

If you agree to participate, you will:

1. **Access the Echoes Phone platform** (a web-based AI character simulation) for approximately 2 weeks
2. **Complete probe tasks** — daily self-documentation activities including screenshots, short text reflections, and optional voice notes submitted via your preferred messaging app (WhatsApp / Telegram / Signal)
3. **Complete a brief pre-study questionnaire** (~5 minutes) and a post-study questionnaire (~10 minutes)
4. **Participate in one interview** (~45–60 minutes) conducted via video call or in-person, scheduled at your convenience after the 2-week period

**Estimated total time commitment**: 3–4 hours over 3 weeks (including ~15–30 min/day of platform use).

### Voluntary Participation

Your participation is **entirely voluntary**. You may:
- Withdraw from the study at any time
- Skip any task or question you are uncomfortable with
- Stop using the platform at any point

Withdrawing will not affect your relationship with the researcher or Aalto University. If you withdraw, you may request that your data be deleted.

### Compensation

- **Full completion** (probe tasks + interview): €20 gift card
- **Partial completion** (probe tasks only, no interview): €10 gift card

### Data Collection & Use

**What data is collected:**
- Your submitted probe materials (text, screenshots, voice notes)
- Questionnaire responses
- Interview recording and transcript (with your consent)
- Optional: anonymized interaction logs from the platform

**How data is used:**
- All data is used solely for academic research purposes (master's thesis and potential academic publications)
- Your identity will be anonymized — you will be referred to by a pseudonym in all reports
- Direct quotes may be used, but will not contain identifying information
- I will not tell anybody who you are; you cannot be identified from this research

### Data Storage & Security

- All digital data is stored on encrypted, EU-based servers (Aalto University infrastructure)
- Physical notes (if any) are stored in a locked cabinet at the researcher's office
- Data is accessible only to the researcher and their thesis supervisor
- API keys and personal account information will be removed from any stored data

### Data Retention & Destruction

- Raw data will be retained for a maximum of **12 months** after the completion of the study (expected: July 2027)
- After this period, all data will be permanently deleted/destroyed
- You may request early deletion of your data at any time by contacting the researcher

### Secondary Use of Data

If insights from this research are used for future product development or commercial purposes:
- You will be contacted for **additional consent** before any such use
- Your anonymized data will not be shared with third parties without explicit permission
- If you do not wish your data to be considered for secondary use, please indicate this in the consent section below

### Content Advisory

The Echoes Phone platform includes AI-generated content that may include:
- Mature themes (romance, emotional conflict, adult topics)
- Fictional violence or distressing scenarios
- Characters expressing strong emotions or personal struggles

You are free to set boundaries via the platform's "Custom Rules" feature, and you may skip any interaction that makes you uncomfortable.

### GDPR Rights (EU Regulation 2016/679)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation, you have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Rectify inaccurate data
- Erase your data ("right to be forgotten")
- Restrict processing of your data
- Data portability
- Object to data processing
- Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

For questions about data protection, contact: [Researcher email] or Aalto University Data Protection Officer: [dpo@aalto.fi]

## 中文版本

### 研究目的

本研究旨在探索用户在通过模拟手机界面与 AI 角色互动时的情感参与体验。本研究为阿尔托大学艺术、设计与建筑学院硕士论文的一部分。

### 参与内容

如果您同意参与，您将：

1. **使用 Echoes Phone 平台**（基于网页的 AI 角色模拟系统）约 2 周
2. **完成探针任务** — 每日自我记录活动，包括截图、简短文字反思和可选的语音记录，通过您偏好的即时通讯工具（WhatsApp / Telegram / Signal）提交

4. **参与一次访谈**（约 45–60 分钟），在 2 周使用期结束后，通过视频通话或面对面方式进行

**预计总时间投入**：3 周内约 3–4 小时（包括每天约 15–30 分钟的平台使用）。

您的参与**完全自愿**。您可以：

退出不会影响您与研究者或阿尔托大学的关系。如果您退出，可以要求删除您的数据。
