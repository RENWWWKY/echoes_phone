# Echoes Phone — Getting Started Guide

> Welcome, and thank you for participating in this study! This guide will help you get started with the Echoes Phone platform.

---

## What is Echoes Phone?

Echoes Phone is a web-based AI phone simulation where you can interact with a fictional character through a realistic phone interface. The character has their own life — they chat with you, write diary entries, browse the internet, listen to music, visit places, and more. Think of it as peeking into someone's digital life and having a conversation with them.

**This is not a productivity tool.** It's an experience — more like reading an interactive novel or playing a narrative game than using an app.

---

## How to Access the Platform

1. Open the link provided by the researcher in **Google Chrome** (recommended)
2. You'll see a **lock screen** with a clock and an avatar area
3. You have two options to enter:
   - **Import a character card** (recommended): Use the JSON file provided by the researcher, OR
   - **Use the creation assistant**: Type a one-line character description and let the AI generate a full character for you
   - **Quick enter**: Click "Enter directly" to explore without a character (limited experience)

### Importing a Character Card (Recommended)

1. Download the `character.json` file provided by the researcher
2. On the lock screen, click the upload area or drag the file onto it
3. The system will parse the character and show a preview
4. Click to confirm and unlock the terminal

### Using the Creation Assistant

1. On the lock screen, find the "Creation Assistant" option
2. Type a brief description, e.g.:
   - "A melancholic librarian who loves jazz"
   - "A cheerful barista who moved to a new city alone"
3. Wait for the AI to generate the full character card
4. Review the preview and click "Apply" to enter

Once unlocked, you'll see a **home screen** with app icons:

| Icon | Name | What it does |

| 🏠 | Smart Home | See where your character is and what they're doing |

**Tips:**
- You can **drag and rearrange** app icons on the home screen
- There are **two pages** — swipe or use the dots at the bottom to switch
- The **vinyl record widget** (top-left) is a quick-access music player
- Click any app icon to open it; click outside or press back to return to home

## Setting Up the API Connection

Before chatting, you need to configure the AI backend:

1. Open **Settings** (⚙️)
2. Enter the **API Base URL**, **API Key**, and **Model name** provided by the researcher
3. Click "Test Connection" — you should see a green success message
4. Close settings

> ⚠️ **Important**: Do not share the API key. It's provided for this study only.

## Your First Chat

1. Open the **Chat** app (💬)
2. You'll see the character's opening message (their "first message")
3. Type anything to start chatting!
4. The character will respond with text, and sometimes emojis, voice messages, or images

### Chat Features to Explore

- **Chat styles**: Switch between SMS (short messages), Novel (narrative prose), and Script (action descriptions in brackets) — change in Settings
- **Voice messages**: Click the microphone to send voice; the character may also send voice
- **Emojis/stickers**: The character has their own sticker collection
- **Transfers**: Send virtual money to the character (they can accept or reject)
- **Location sharing**: Share a location in chat

### What to Notice

- How does the character's tone feel? Natural? Robotic? Charming?
- Do the "status snapshots" (outfit, action, inner thoughts) add to the experience?
- Does the character remember things from earlier conversations?
- How does the character respond to emotional or personal topics?

## Exploring Other Apps

You don't need to use every app — explore what interests you. But here are some highlights:

### Smart Home (🏠)
- See your character's current location and what they're doing
- If you leave and come back after 2+ hours, the system generates your character's offline activities
- This is where you see the character "living their life" independently

### Diary (📔)
- The character writes diary entries about their thoughts and experiences
- These are private — the character doesn't know you're reading them
- Notice how diary tone differs from chat tone

### Music (🎵)
- The AI recommends songs based on the conversation mood
- Features a vinyl record player UI with lyrics display
- Can you notice a connection between the music and your recent chats?

### Browser (🌐)
- View the character's search history — both normal and private/incognito
- This reveals what the character is curious or worried about
- Note: incognito searches may include sensitive content

### Traces (🧾)
- Shopping receipts that reflect the character's lifestyle
- Items may relate to conversations you've had
- Check the character's "inner thought" on each purchase

## Important Notes

- **All data is stored locally** in your browser. Clearing browser data will erase everything.
- **The character is AI-generated.** They are not a real person. Responses are generated by language models.
- **Content may be mature.** The platform is designed for adult users. Characters may discuss sensitive topics.
- **You control the boundaries.** Use "Custom Rules" in Settings to set limits on what the character can discuss.
- **The platform works best on desktop Chrome.** Mobile is not fully supported.
