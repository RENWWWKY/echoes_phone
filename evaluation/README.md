## Methodology Summary

**Approach**: Digital Design Probes (Empathy Probes variant)
- Fully digital probe kit (no physical artefacts)
- Remote deployment via participants' preferred messaging platforms
- 7-day self-documentation period
- Followed by 45–60 min material-led semi-structured interviews

**Research Questions** (from thesis):
- **RQ1**: How can AI companionship technologies be designed to support emotional disclosure and identity negotiation while maintaining user agency?
- **RQ2**: Under what interaction conditions do human-AI relationships foster genuine identity growth rather than devolving into passive, closed-world dynamics?
- **RQ3**: In what ways does the design and implementation of Echoes Phone instantiate these principles to help co-creation and reciprocal relationships?

**Analytical framework**: Evaluated against three structural criteria for openness:
1. **Genuine otherness** — does the AI contribute independent perspective?
2. **Productive friction** — does the interaction generate resistance rather than frictionless comfort?
3. **Ecological connection** — does the sandbox maintain permeability to the user's broader social world?

**Key design decisions**:
1. Tasks organized by reflection theme, NOT by feature — users explore freely from Day 1
2. Focus on emotional/relational experience, not functional usability
3. Flexible data submission via WhatsApp/Telegram/Signal
4. Semi-structured interview uses participant's own probe materials as conversation starters
5. 12–14 recruited, expect 10+ completing full cycle (~50% retention assumption)
