## How to Submit

### Text
Just type your response in the chat. You can write in Chinese or English.

### Screenshots
1. Take a screenshot on your computer:
   - **Mac**: `Cmd + Shift + 4` (select area) or `Cmd + Shift + 3` (full screen)
   - **Windows**: `Win + Shift + S` (select area) or `PrtSc` (full screen)
2. Send the image file directly in the chat
3. Add a brief caption if helpful (e.g., "This was the diary entry that surprised me")

### Voice Notes
1. Open your messaging app
2. Hold the voice record button and speak
3. Keep it under 60 seconds (split into multiple notes if needed)
4. Release to send

### Creative Outputs
- **Hand drawings**: Take a photo with your phone and send it
- **Digital creations**: Screenshot or export and send the file
- **Collages**: Screenshot or photo

## Submission Format Tips

### When submitting a reflection, try to include:
1. **Context**: Which task are you responding to? (e.g., "Day 3 — diary task")
2. **Content**: Your reflection text, screenshot, or voice note
3. **Feeling** (optional): An emoji that captures your mood 😊😐🤔😮😤

### Example text submission:
> Day 3 — Diary task
> The diary entry where [character name] wrote about their childhood was really unexpected. It made me feel like I was reading someone's real journal. A bit guilty, but also really drawn in. 🤔

### Example voice note:
> "So I just read the character's diary and honestly I felt kind of weird about it? Like, I know it's AI but the writing felt so personal. I almost wanted to apologize for reading it."

## Frequency

- **Daily reflection**: One submission per day (after completing that day's task)
- **Spontaneous thoughts**: Anytime! If something happens during your use that strikes you, send a quick note
- **Creative tasks**: One submission on Day 10 and Day 12

---

## Privacy & Data Handling

- Your submissions are stored securely on encrypted servers
- Only the researcher and thesis supervisor have access
- Your identity is protected by a pseudonym in all reports
- You can request deletion of any submission at any time
- All data will be destroyed within 12 months of study completion

---

## Missed a Day?

No problem. Just continue with the next day's task. You can also combine reflections from multiple days into one submission. The goal is to capture your experience, not to punish you for missing a day.
