# Echoes Phone — Participant Briefing Script

> Use this script when first contacting potential participants (email, message, or in-person).

---

## Initial Contact Message

Subject: Invitation to participate in a research study about AI character interaction

---

Hi [Name],

I'm [Your Name], a master's student at Aalto University. I'm conducting a research study on how people experience emotional engagement with AI-generated fictional characters, and I'm looking for participants.

**What the study involves:**
- Using a web-based AI character simulation platform called "Echoes Phone" for about 2 weeks (~15–30 min/day)
- Completing daily reflection tasks (text, screenshots, or voice notes — submitted via WhatsApp/Telegram/Signal)
- Filling out two short questionnaires (before and after)
- One interview (45–60 min, via video call or in-person)

**Time commitment**: ~3–4 hours total over 3 weeks

**Compensation**: €20 gift card for full completion (€10 for partial)

**What you'll need**: A desktop computer with Chrome, internet access, and a messaging app (WhatsApp/Telegram/Signal)

**Important notes:**
- The platform includes AI-generated content that may be mature in nature
- Your data will be anonymized and stored securely
- You can withdraw at any time without penalty

If you're interested or have questions, just reply to this message and I'll send you the detailed information sheet and consent form.

Thanks for considering this!

[Your Name]
[Email]
[Phone/WhatsApp]

## Follow-up: When Someone Expresses Interest

Great, thanks for your interest! Here's what happens next:

1. I'll send you a **consent form** — please read it carefully and let me know if you have questions
2. Once you've signed, I'll send you the **Getting Started Guide** and the **pre-study questionnaire**
3. I'll share the platform link, a character card file, and the API setup details
4. We'll agree on a **start date** for your 2-week period
5. I'll be available on [messaging platform] throughout the study for questions and support

The study period would be [date range]. Does that work for you?

## Reminders During the Study

### Day 1 (if no submission received):
Hey [Name]! Just checking in — have you had a chance to get started with Echoes Phone? Let me know if you need help with setup or have any questions. No rush!

### Mid-study (Day 7):
Hi [Name]! You're halfway through — thanks for your submissions so far. How's the experience going? Any questions or issues? Remember, there's no pressure to do every task perfectly — just share what feels natural.

### End of Week 2:
Hi [Name]! Today's the last day of the study period. Thanks so much for your participation! Once you've completed the post-study questionnaire, I'll reach out to schedule your interview. Let me know if you have any final thoughts to share.
